Nishant Shukla

Graduate Student at UCLA

I am a Computer Science Masters student continuing as a Ph.D. candidate. My research interests are Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and Knowledge Representation.

At the University of Virginia, I graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science, and a B.A. in Mathematics. There, I was a founding member of HackUVA. I also lectured a course on Haskell.

Currently, I work at the Center for Vision, Cognition, Learning, and Art with Dr. Caiming Xiong and Professor Song-Chun Zhu.
